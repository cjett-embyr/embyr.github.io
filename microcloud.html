Microcloud

Microcloud is the webdomain of PULSAR Laboratories, a community-driven, crowd-funded, grassroots research organization with the express goal of allowing those interested to pursue topics and projects interesting to them. We facilitate this by providing equipment, workspace, and advice from experienced members, in order to help new projects get off the ground and to bring new ideas to fruition.
